Key Features

Custom Time Slot Analysis

Top Volatility Window (On-Chart)

Display a live on-chart window showing the top N most volatile time slots with average, max, and observation counts—updated every N bars.

Per-Bar Size Labels

Optionally show labels under each bar displaying size in Ticks, Points, or Both—with configurable displacement and color.

RTH Filtering

Use UseRthWindow to filter slot statistics to Regular Trading Hours only, or leave it off to analyze the full session.

Day-of-Week Selection

Enable/disable specific days (Sunday–Saturday) to focus your analysis on the trading days that matter most to your strategy.

CSV Export

Export a detailed CSV file with all time slots ranked by average bar size, including max values, timestamps, and observation counts.

How It Works

1

Define Your Time Slots

Set SlotSeconds (e.g., 30 seconds) to divide the trading day into custom windows. The indicator tracks each slot's average bar size (High-Low in ticks).

2

Collect Historical Data

As bars close, the indicator accumulates statistics for each time slot across all loaded history, optionally filtering by RTH and selected days of the week.

3

Display Top Volatility Window

Enable ShowTopVolatilityWindow to see a live on-chart ranking of the top N most volatile time slots, updated every N bars with average, max, and observation counts.

4

Export & Analyze

When the indicator reaches real-time or terminates, it prints the top N slots to the Output window and optionally exports a full CSV report for deeper analysis.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does MQT_BarSizeStudy analyze?

It divides the trading day into custom time slots (defined by SlotSeconds) and ranks each slot by average bar size (High-Low in ticks) across all loaded historical data.

How do I filter the analysis to Regular Trading Hours only?

Enable UseRthWindow and set RthStartTime and RthEndTime to your desired RTH window. Only bars within this window will be included in the slot statistics.

Can I see the results on the chart in real-time?

Yes. Enable ShowTopVolatilityWindow to display a live on-chart window showing the top N most volatile time slots, updated every N bars with average, max, and observation counts.

What are the per-bar labels?

When ShowBarSizeLabels is enabled, the indicator displays the size of each bar (High-Low) under the bar. You can choose to display in Ticks, Points, or Both.

Can I filter labels independently from statistics?

Yes. LabelOnlyDuringRth filters only the labels (independent of the stats filter). You can also use LabelEveryNBars to reduce label frequency.

How do I export the results?

Enable ExportCsv and specify a CsvFileName. When the indicator reaches real-time or terminates, it exports a CSV file to your NinjaTrader user data directory with all time slots ranked by average bar size.

Can I analyze specific days of the week only?

Yes. Use the Days group parameters (RunSunday, RunMonday, etc.) to enable/disable specific days. Only bars from enabled days will be included in the analysis.

What slot size should I use?

It depends on your trading style. Scalpers might use 30-60 seconds, while swing traders might use 300-600 seconds (5-10 minutes). Experiment with different values to find the optimal window for your strategy.
